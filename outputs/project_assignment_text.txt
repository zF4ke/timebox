  The  project  aims  to  explore  several  components  of  Multi-Agent  System  (MAS),  including  coordination,  
decision-making,

learning.
 
Students  are  free  to  choose  the  programming  language  to  develop  their  system.  
 
PROJECTS  We  can  have  2  types  of  projects  focusing  on:  
●  the  reasoning  of  the  agents  (game-theory  or  multi-agent  learning);  
●  orchestration,  coordination  or  collaboration  between  agents;  
For  any  of  these  options,  you  should  identify  a  scientific  paper  or  existing  work  and  reproduce  it.  
Reproducing  is  neither  a  complete  reproduction  (might  be  too  difficult  or  too  extensive)  nor  restricting

EVALUATION  CRITERIA   
Grading  will  be  based  on  the  following  criteria:  
●  description  of  the  problem;  
●  identification  of  limitations  of  the  work;  
●  critical  comparison  between  the  implementation  alternatives  included  advantages  and  
disadvantages;
 
●  identification  of  limitations  of  the  solutions  provided  and  possible  solutions;  
●  identification  of  limitations  of  the  results  and  simulations;  
Your  implementation  does  not  need  to  be  perfect,  but  you  should  identify  how  you  could  improve  it,  and  
what
 
was
 
done
 
due
 
to
 
some
 
practical
 
limitations.
